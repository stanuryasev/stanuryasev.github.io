CASE STUDY SUMMARYnThis case study solves the retirement portfolio selection problem. The objective is to maximize discounted terminal wealth of the investor, while maintaining constant cash outflows from the portfolio by selling some portion of assets, over an entire investment horizon. The cash outflow requirements are imposed using CVaR constraints and additionally, by monotonicity constraint on the cash outflows.
