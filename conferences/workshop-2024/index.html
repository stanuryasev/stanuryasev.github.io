Workshop 2024

Third Workshop on Recent Trends in Machine Learning and Risk Management

Date: October 17-18 (Thursday-Friday), 2024
Venue: Stony Brook University, Math Tower, Room S-240
Participation: In-person

Program

Please see the full program and book of abstracts here.

Workshop Outline:

This workshop will bring together top academics and practitioners in Reinforcement Learning, Stochastic Optimization, Statistical Learning and Machine Learning. The objective is to exchange recent ideas and approaches focusing on numerical methods and applications in:
• Risk Management,
• Portfolio Optimization,
• High-Frequency Trading
• Derivatives Pricing and Hedging
