Workshop: New Ideas in Quantitative Finance

Date: November 4-5, 2019  
Venue: Stony Brook University (S-24Contact Information: Math Tower), Stony Brook, NY

Workshop Outline

This workshop will bring together top academics and practitioners in Quantitative Finance area. The objective is to exchange recent ideas and approaches focusing on numerical methods and applications. Possible topics include:

    n
  • Portfolio optimization
    • n
  • Pricing of financial securities
    • n
  • Risk management
    • n
  • Machine learning approaches, such as, neural networks and classification
    • n
  • Forecasting of distributions
n
