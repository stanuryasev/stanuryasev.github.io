Workshop: Applications of Risk-Averse Stochastic Optimization in Engineering Systems

n

Date: December 15-16, 2025  
Venue: University of Florida, Little Hall (LIT), Room 225, Gainesville, FL, URL: LIT

Workshop Outline

This workshop brings together experts in risk-averse stochastic optimization, numerical analysis, operations research, and statistics to explore efficient modeling and algorithmic frameworks for uncertainty-aware design and control. In particular the focus is on:

• Topology Optimization under Uncertainty
n• Applications of Stochastic Optimization in Statistics
n• PDE-Constrained Stochastic Optimization
n• Mixed-Integer Stochastic Optimization
n• Stochastic Dynamic Programming

nWorkshop presentations are by invitation only.n
