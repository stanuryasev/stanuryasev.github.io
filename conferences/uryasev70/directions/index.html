Directions

Stony Brook University is located on the north shore of Long Island, about 50 miles east of New York City. It is easily reached by train or car. See the Campus Directions and the Campus Map.

The conference will be held in the Math Tower building, Room S-240, located next to the Simons Center for Geometry and Physics. You can view directions to the Math Tower on Google Maps here. You can also search for “Simons Center for Geometry and Physics” in Google or Apple Maps.

Hotel Information

A block of rooms is reserved for conference participants at the Hilton Garden Inn Stony Brook at a discounted rate. Booking details will be posted here closer to the event.
